**_AI is the ability of the machine or computer to think, learn and make decisions like humans._**

It is a field of computer science focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.
It enables machines to understand, reason, learn and respond intelligently to various types of inputs.
AI systems can simulate human-like cognitive functions such as perception, decision-making, problem solving and even creativity, it is done using multiple [[AI Techniques]].

These tasks include:
- Learning from experience
- Understanding Language
- Recognizing images or speech
- Making decisions
- Solving Problems

[[History of AI]] is quite interesting.

Types of AI :
1. **[[Narrow AI]]** : AI designed to complete very specific actions; unable to independently learn. 
2. **[[Artificial General Intelligence]]** : AI designed to learn, think and perform at similar levels to humans.
3. **[[Artificial Superintelligence]]** : AI able to surpass the knowledge and capabilities of humans

Components of AI :
1. **Learning** : Ability to learn from data.
2. **Reasoning** : Ability to make logical decisions.
3. **Problem Solving** : AI find the best solution among many possibilities.
4. **Perception** : Ability to understand sensory input.
5. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** : Ability to understand human language.

Functionality-Based Types of Artificial Intelligence
1. **Reactive Machine AI** : Capable of responding to external stimuli in real time, unable to build memory or store information for future.
2. **Limited Memory AI** : Can store knowledge and use it to learn and train for future tasks.
3. **Theory of Mind AI** : Can sense and respond to human emotions, performs the tasks of limited memory machines.
4. **Self-Aware AI** : Can recognize others' emotions, has sense of self and human-level intelligence.
