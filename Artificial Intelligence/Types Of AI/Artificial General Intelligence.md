 Level of artificial intelligence that matches human intelligence in understanding, learning, and problem-solving.
It's also know as **Strong AI**.

What an AI needs to become AGI : 
![[Pasted image 20260102211752.png]]

Characteristics:
1. **Versatility** : Transcends the limitations of Narrow AI by excelling at multiple tasks.
2. **Adaptability** : Learn from past experiences and apply that knowledge to unfamiliar scenarios.
3. **Self-improvement** : Capacity for autonomous enhancement. It can identify its strengths and weaknesses, refine its strategies, and even innovate new approaches to problem-solving without human intervention.
4. **General Understanding** : Confined to predefined parameters, AGI comprehends and interacts with the world in a flexible, human-like manner, processing and interpreting data across diverse contexts.
5. Multimodal Capabilities : It is expected to understand and process complex data across various senses and contexts, similar to human perception.
