**AI systems designed and trained to perform specific tasks or limited range of function with pre-defined constraints**.
Also known as **Weak AI**.
Operates within a predefined scope, excelling in the specific domains it has been programmed to handle.

Characteristics of Narrow AI : 
1. **Specialization** : Built to handle a specific problem.
2. **Data Dependency** : Relies on the quality and quantity of the data it is trained on.
3. **Lack of Self-Awareness** : Do not posses consciousness of self-awareness.
4. **Reactive Nature** : Reacts to the specific inputs based on their training.
5. **Task-optimization** : Optimized for efficiency and accuracy in a particular domain.
