
1. 1950 : Foundation
- **Alan Turing** proposed the idea of intelligent machines.
- He introduced the **Turing Test** also known as **Imitation game**.
- In Turing test, a human evaluator judges a text transcript of a natural-language conversation between a human and a machine. The evaluator tries to identify the machine, and the machine passes if the evaluator cannot reliably tell them apart.

2. 1956 : Birth of AI
- The term Artificial Intelligence is introduced by **John McCarthy** at the Dartmouth Conference.

3. 1970 - 1990 : AI Winter
- Progress slowed due to lack of computing power and data.

4. 2000 : Modern AI
- Growth of Machine Learning, Big Data and Deep Learning.

5. 2010 - Present
- Rapid development in :
	- Chatbots 
	- Autonomous Vehicles 
	- Generative AI
