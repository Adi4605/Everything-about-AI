A tree-like diagram that visualizes the relationships between similar objects, often used to show the results of hierarchical clustering.

It shows how individual data points or groups merge in a hierarchical structure, with the height of the branches indicating the distance or dissimilarity between the clusters.

Key components

- **Leaves:** The individual data points at the bottom of the diagram.
- **Branches:** The lines that connect the leaves and clusters.
- **Height:** The vertical axis often represents the distance or dissimilarity at which clusters are merged.
